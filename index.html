Cruising the Canyon is a Bryce 3D guidebook filled with explanations on the fundamentals, useful functions, tips and tricks, and applications filled with the finest renders all made in-program. The guide was made to be a modern written documentation of Bryce 3D, making it useful not just for beginner but for all Bryce creators. Plus sections of the book cover useful programs, Bryce alternatives, and helpful tutorials. And best of all - it's free!
